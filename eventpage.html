Arrive Early and Enjoy the Festival Art Show!

Laguna Beach is a popular summer destination which leads to increased traffic and full parking lots. The best way to avoid the stress? Arrive early!

There’s plenty to see and do at the Festival Art Show before the Pageant. Peruse the art show, create art in our workshops, enjoy a glass of wine, dine at one of our restaurants and enjoy a live concert!

Plus, we’re only 5 minutes walking distance from downtown Laguna Beach.

Dress Appropriately

Although you might think sunny and warm Laguna Beach weather, be prepared for the temperature to drop when the sun goes down. We’d suggest bringing a blanket or sweater with you or purchasing one from our gift shop.

What to Bring

Many patrons who return annually to the Pageant bring these items with them to enhance their experience:

  • Binoculars
  • Jacket or blanket
  • Seat cushions

You may also rent binoculars and seat cushions on site. Jackets and blankets are available to purchase at our gift shop next to the front gates.

Theater Etiquette

As a courtesy to our volunteers and the surrounding audience, please refrain from talking during the performance. Silence or turn off all electronic devices, including cell phones, beepers and watch alarms. If you need assistance during the show, please go to your nearest usher or security guard. Be polite to all fellow patrons and Festival/Pageant staff.

Photography

No photography or audio/video recording of any kind are permitted during the Pageant.

What Not to Bring

Items that are prohibited and should be left at home include:

  • Candles
  • Selfie Sticks
  • Laser pointers
  • Personal furniture including chairs and tables
See a complete list in our House Rules.
